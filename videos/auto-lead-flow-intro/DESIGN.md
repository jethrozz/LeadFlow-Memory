# Auto Lead Flow Video Design

## Style Prompt

Formal B2B SaaS motion graphics for an AI/Web3 infrastructure demo. The video should feel precise, credible, and product-led: dark teal canvas, clean editorial typography, thin signal lines, mobile outreach motifs, and verifiable artifact cards. Motion should be smooth and purposeful, with GSAP-driven staggered entrances, left-to-right lead-flow movement, and restrained cyan/coral highlights.

## Colors

- Background: `#071F1E`
- Surface: `#F4F8F7`
- Ink: `#113B39`
- Muted text: `#A9C2BE`
- Signal cyan: `#14B8C4`
- Deep teal: `#0E7D72`
- Outreach coral: `#FF6F4F`
- Proof gold: `#F5C66B`

## Typography

- Display/title: `Georgia`, serif, weight 700
- Body: `Arial`, sans-serif, weight 350-700
- Data labels: `Courier New`, monospace, weight 500-700

## Motion

- Scene pacing: 6-8 seconds each, total target 42 seconds.
- Transitions: primary soft push/crossfade between scenes, no jump cuts.
- Entrances: every scene element animates in with varied `power3.out`, `expo.out`, `back.out`, and `sine.out` eases.
- Ambient motion: low-opacity rings, traces, and signal dots breathe or drift with finite repeat counts.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use cartoon robots or mascot-style agent visuals.
- Do not use generic CRM dashboard stock imagery.
- Do not use full-screen purple/blue gradients.
- Do not overload the video with technical implementation details.
- Do not include Chinese text in the video.
